Frequently asked questions

How does Ableten work?

Ableten combines a Chrome extension and web app. Highlight articles as you read, save screenshots, extract text from images with OCR, organize everything into research collections, and generate citations or draft writing from your saved sources.

Can Ableten extract text from screenshots?

Yes. Ableten supports OCR for screenshots and images, turning image-based research into searchable text you can organize and cite alongside web highlights.

Does Ableten support APA and MLA citations?

Yes. Ableten generates citations and bibliographies from your saved highlights and sources, including APA and MLA formats.

Can I organize research into collections?

Yes. Highlights, notes, and OCR extracts are automatically organized into research collections so you can find related material quickly.

Is Ableten free?

Ableten offers a free Chrome extension to get started. Sign up at app.ableten.xyz to access your research library and web app. Questions? Visit our support center.
