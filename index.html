Frequently asked questions

What is Ableten?

Ableten is your Source Library for everything worth saving. It helps you capture recommendations and research from the web, organize them into collections, and turn them into trip plans, bibliographies, or grounded drafts.

How is Ableten different from generic AI writing tools?

Most tools start from a blank prompt. Ableten starts from sources you've already saved—links, highlights, screenshots, and notes—so outputs are grounded in your material, not AI guesses.

Can I use Ableten for trip planning?

Yes. Collect friend recommendations and saved places, then compose a shareable itinerary with citations back to original recs. See our trip planner page.

Can Ableten extract text from screenshots?

Yes. Forward images and links to email capture, or save while browsing. Text and map links can be extracted and organized in collections.

Does Ableten support citations and bibliographies?

Yes. Generate APA and MLA bibliographies from saved sources and use Tag References in Word via the Chrome extension.

Is Ableten free?

The Chrome extension is free to get started. Sign up at app.ableten.xyz to open your Source Library. Questions? Visit our support center.
