Features

Highlight and save your insights as you read

Use Ableten's Chrome extension to highlight and save your insights as you read using the "Copy" function. You can then access your highlights from your browser or webapp.

Auto-organize your highlights into collections

Your highlights are automatically organized into collections based on the content of the highlights so that you can easily find them later.

Generate thought articles that are original and evidence-based

Quickly generate thought articles that are original and evidence-based from your highlights. You can then export your thought articles as markdown files or Word documents.
